== Description ==

Team Vcard Generator lets you create personal cards of members of the company and then get a personalized URL and a QR-Code for each.
Visitors to your site can download the contact on their Android, Iphone or Outlook in VCF format.
You can include this url on your business card.



== Installation ==

1. Upload `team-vcard-generator.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1.
